Identica: Shader Replacement Dance Event


The identica dance event takes place Friday February 27th 2026. This is an experimental two hour event, with an emphasis on body language, movement, and light.

This world uses a complex system of cameras and layers to render the entire 3D environment with one shader.

When your shader is replaced, your main texture and color will be used, but any other settings or textures will be ignored, and every enabled mesh will render opaque.

If you have glasses or other materials you need to be invisible, you can download the shader below and enable the shader keyword _MODE_ZEROPOS_ON . If you're already happy with how you look in the test world below, you're good to go!
